Solar Powered RC

A Solar Robot is basically a Robot that uses the solar energy instead of Battery Power. The electricity robots use a lot of electricity to get it going.Solar Robots work by the energy of the sun. Below are some of the features of this project

  • No use of Batteries to run the Robot
  • Concept of clean and green Solar Energy
  • Science educational, Safe & Eco-friendly
  • Use of Science and latest Solar Technology
  • Concept of machanical device

This project is about utilising alternate source of energy which is the future!

Solar Powered Robotic Car

The objective of this project is to run Robot through the solar power. In this project Solar power is used to drive a small motor inside this Robot. Below are the component used in making this project.

  • Solar Panel with Springs
  • Motor with Connectors
  • Gear with Shaft
  • Plastic Material for Robot

    • How to make Solar Control Robotic Car

  • You need to assemble the Robot as per the Manuals.
  • Connect the Solar Panel with the small motor inside the Robot.

    • How Solar Control Robotic Car works

  • Solar panel works by allowing photons, or particles of light, to knock electrons free from atoms, generating a flow of electricity.
  • Solar panels actually comprise many, smaller units called photovoltaic cells. (Photovoltaic simply convert sunlight into electricity).
  • The flow of Electricity in Solar Panel , drives small motor.
  • Motor rotates the Gear and Shaft connected with wheel.
  • Robot moves around once it places in the Sun.
