Course Information

  • Eligibility Engineering, BSC(CS), MSC(CS), BCA, MCA & School Student (class 8 - class 12)
  • Project Robotic Car
  • Total Students Maximum 5 Student per batch
  • Course Duration 8 Weeks (Once in a week)
  • Class Duration 2 Hrs
  • Course Start Ongoing
  • Course Price Contact for more details
  • Kit Price Not Included, Student may purchase own Kit

Description

This intermediate level Robotics course.

In this course we shall help to improve knowledge and interest among the students on Robotics science. This will also help the students to enhance their mental and practical ability while building Robotic Car. The course includes complete knowledge of basic electronics, use of electronic devices, use of electronics components , Microcontroller and Programming in embedded C. In this course student will learn about Embedded systems , hardware and software interface and design and making Robotics product
